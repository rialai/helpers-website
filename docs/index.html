Software vendors around a microbusiness

These vendors shape how a microbusiness operates internally, faces the market externally, and reaches B2C demand through third-party platforms.

Internal Operations

  • Accounting / ERP: 1C, QuickBooks, Xero, ERPNext
  • Payments / POS / invoicing: Stripe, Square, SumUp, PayPal, Revolut Business
  • CRM / lead management: HubSpot, Zoho, Pipedrive
  • Communications: WhatsApp Business, Telegram, email platforms, telephony, helpdesk tools
  • Scheduling / booking: Calendly, Fresha, Treatwell, Booksy, Mindbody
  • Website / store / CMS: Shopify, WordPress, Wix, Tilda, Ecwid
  • Logistics / delivery: Shippo, EasyPost, courier platforms, marketplace logistics
  • HR / payroll: Gusto, Deel, local payroll vendors

External Market Edge

  • Search discovery: Google Search, Google Maps
  • Social demand: Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, YouTube
  • Advertising platforms: Google Ads, Meta Ads, TikTok Ads
  • Review / trust: Google Reviews, Trustpilot, Yelp, TripAdvisor
  • Messaging bridges: WhatsApp, Instagram DMs, Facebook Messenger, Telegram
  • Payment trust layer: Stripe Checkout, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, Klarna

B2C Bridge Platforms

  • Marketplaces: Amazon, Etsy, eBay, Wildberries, Ozon, Avito
  • Service marketplaces: Upwork, Fiverr
  • Travel / hospitality: Booking.com, Airbnb
  • Food delivery: Deliveroo, Uber Eats
  • Aggregators: price comparison sites, booking aggregators, food aggregators, service directories
  • What they control: discovery, trust, checkout, routing, and repeat access to the customer

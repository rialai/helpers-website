How it works

A practical 5-step operating cycle that keeps incentives aligned for both the member and the cooperative.

    What members receive

    Support is not just lead flow. It combines acquisition setup, process structure, and shared operational visibility.

    Membership and commission

    Annual contribution is voluntary. It lowers commission and influences internal priority for active growth support.

    Membership contribution is voluntary and intended to be paid after real value is delivered. If a member does not contribute, they remain in the cooperative community and can learn from the network. Active growth effort and internal visibility are prioritized toward members who sustain the system. Faster commission return improves visibility, and at the highest contribution level, paid-year history can also influence priority.

    Priority and visibility logic

    This is an internal reputation model. It rewards responsible delivery, transparent records, and cooperative contribution.

02 Audience

A platform for entrepreneurs. Designed primarily for small and medium-sized businesses — tradespeople and handymen — but equally valuable for entrepreneurial managers inside larger organisations, and for people who, while not doing hands-on work themselves, can perform functional analysis of the systems that tradespeople need in order to focus exclusively on their craft.

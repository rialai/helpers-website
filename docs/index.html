Who are helpers?

Ireland has roughly 272,500 small and medium enterprises — over 99% of all companies in the country. Advertising platforms profit from gaps in handymen's schedules. But what is the nature of that gap? Is it a lucky opening — or a pattern of mistakes that keeps creating empty time?

Overpriced or badly chosen materials, missed deadlines while chasing false goals at the client's expense, passing responsibility to a careless subordinate... Buying ads is a great way out if you systematically do mistakes at work!

No problem. Pay the advertising bills and get a new victim. That is how marketplaces and contextual advertising work: in most cases, the people hunting for you are the ones who cannot sustain an organically and continuously booked schedule. And how do you separate the person who says he can do the work from the one who actually does it every day?
